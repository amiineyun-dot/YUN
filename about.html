WHO WE ARE

Est. 2024 — Algeria

THE STORY

Born from the static of a broken CRT screen and the glow of early-2000s flip phones, YUN is not just a brand — it's a frequency. We decode the DNA of Y2K fashion and remix it for a generation that never lived through it, but feels it in their bones.

Every piece we curate is a bridge between eras — metallic textures meet digital minimalism, cargo pockets hold AirPods, and butterfly clips are worn with the same reckless confidence as they were in 2002. We don't do nostalgia. We do evolution.

Founded in Algeria, built for the global grid. YUN exists at the intersection of street culture and cyber aesthetic — where North African energy meets the pixelated dreams of the early internet.

FASHION IS CODE. WE WRITE IT.

OUR VALUES

Quality

Every garment is inspected, tested, and verified. We don't drop anything we wouldn't wear ourselves — from the stitching to the packaging.

Authenticity

No fast-fashion copies. Each piece is sourced, designed, or curated with intention. What you wear tells a story — make sure it's a real one.

Digital-First

Born online, built for the scroll. From our social presence to our checkout flow — everything is designed for the way you actually shop.
